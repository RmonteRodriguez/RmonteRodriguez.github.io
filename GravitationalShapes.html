Gravitational Shapes

Overview

Gravitational Shapes is an experimental platformer I made to see how Unity's gravity system works. The goal is to make it to the door on the other side of the room. The player can not jump and can only invert the gravity. They must constantly change the direction of the gravity to make it to the door. Some doors have buttons that need to be pressed in order to open them and some items are not affect by gravity either.

Features

  • Player controller that allows the player to alter gravity
  • Handfull of levels to test the gravity system

Project Design Goals

  • Design a game where the player controls gravity
  • Design small puzzles that the player will need to alter gravity to complete

Tech

  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Tools Used: Unity, Google Draw
  • Team Size: 1

Capstone

Overview

Summary

This was the final project for my college degree. This project lasted around 4 months and I worked on a team of 5 to create a game using another game as a reference. My team chose Genshin Impact however we made it a 2D platformer to make it more unique to us. At the end of each milestone we did sprint reviews to show exactly what we worked on and I have added buttons to go view each one of them. We also created a trailer for the game as well.

Design Goals

  • Design a genshin impact clone
  • Design levels that amplify the abilities of the character and their elemental abilities
  • Design enemies that have elemental abilities
  • Design boss enemies with unique powers compared to normal enemies

What I Worked On

Enemy Slimes

I primarily did most of the elemental slims and their larger varients. The smaller slimes did an elemental damage to the player and would add an elemtal reaction to the player as well. Their larger varients served to be a more difficult version of the slime enemy. Each element had their own elemtal varient and each one had a unique elemtal attack, an example would be the earth slime would jump in the air and slam down doing an AOE earthquake attack.

    Variety of Power Ups

    Dreamscape Runners is a 2D speedrunning platformer where players either race against their own best time or another player in local multiplayer. The player has the ability to go through a handful of handcrafted levels with a variety of obstacles, power ups, and enemies standing in their way to the finish line.

Speedrun Timer

The speedrun timer is used to determine who wins and to keep track of the player's score. At the end of the race the game will store and show the best time so the player could try and beat their previous score. If there are multiple players then it will show the best score of the both of them and that is used to determine who has won.

    Variety of hazards/enemies

    Throughout each level there are a variety of hazards and enemies used to stop or slow down the players. These could be simple slime enemies that could kill the player or pits that could stop the player in their tracks.

Capstone Project

This was the final project for my college degree. This project lasted around 4 months and I worked on a team of 5 to create a game using another game as a reference. My team chose Genshin Impact however we made it a 2D platformer to make it more unique to us. Within this project I worked on most of the enemies, the initial character swapping system, the health/stamina systems as well as a bunch of small features. I designed 2 levels that made it into the final project as well, both levels are themed to fit what we were trying to achieve. At the end of each milestone we did sprint reviews to show exactly what we worked on and I have added buttons to go view each one of them. We also created a trailer for the game as well.

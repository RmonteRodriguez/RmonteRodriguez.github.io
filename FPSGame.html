FPS Game

This project was the second week of working on a team. We were given a few templates and was told to choose one. We chose the FPS template. The features I added were a telepoter that would take the player from an initial teleporter to an end point in. I also added a grappling hook. Once I added my two main mechanics I added a few gamefeel elements, I added sound affects for when the player used the telepoter, used a jump pad and got a power up.

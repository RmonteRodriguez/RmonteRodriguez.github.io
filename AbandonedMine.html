Abandoned Mine

Overview

This level is a fully playable level I made as an assingment. I was restricted to a certain time period and mechanics. The level contains a gate and a valve, the player is also able to climb and pull themselves onto certain platforms. I originally made a moodboard and then made an initial blockmesh. After making that blockmesh I then created another blockmesh. Once I completed the second blockmesh I then added assets to make the level look good.

Features

  • A 3 minute level
  • Climable objects
  • Valves and Gates
  • Collectables

Project Design Goals

  • Design a 3 minute level based on an western town and abandoned mine
  • Design a valve that stops the player going back to older content
  • Design a gate that stops players if they do not meet the minimum requirement

Tech

  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Engine: Unreal
  • Language: Blueprints
  • Tools Used: Unreal
  • Team Size: 1
  • Role: Level Designer, Gameplay Programmer

Dreamscape Runners

Overview

Summary

Dreamscape Runners is a 2D speedrunning platformer where players either race against their own best time or another player in local multiplayer. The player has the ability to go through a handful of handcrafted levels with a variety of obstacles, power ups, and enemies standing in their way to the finish line.

Design Goals

  • Design a simple yet fun game to allow our programmer to adjust to Unity
  • Design a game that had fast-paced gameplay
  • Design a game that also included some form of combat
  • Design levels that use the power ups to get an advantage

What I Worked On

Single/Multiplayer capability

I wanted to add the ability for multiple people to race against eachother. I added in multiplayer capability using Unity's new input system allowing the second player to join on controller while the first player uses the keyboard.

    Variety of Power Ups

    To keep the game interesting and fun I added a variety of powerups which could be used against the enemies and the other players within the race. Some powerups will slow things down, others will protect you, and some may even be used to open parts of the map that is not normally accessible.

Speedrun Timer

We needed a way to score the players so I opted to have a speedrun timer that will be used to show the best time of the players. At the end of the race the times are shown to see who was faster. It will also show the fastest time for the stage as well so players could strive to get better times.

    Variety of hazards/enemies

    Another way we decided make the races more interesting is by adding hazards and enemies. Some examples of hazards I added were spikes that would damage the player and pits that would immediately kill the player. We also have a variety of enemies. We have a slime that would damage the player and slow them down temporarily, we also have cannons that will shoot projectiles at the player.

Dreamscape Runners

Overview

Dreamscape Runners is a 2D speedrunning platformer where players either race against their own best time or another player in local multiplayer. The player has the ability to go through a handful of handcrafted levels with a variety of obstacles, power ups, and enemies standing in their way to the finish line.

Things I Worked On

  • Initial concept and prototyping
  • Prototyped and create player controller and multiplayer controller
  • Designed and programmed five different power ups
  • Created multiple hazards such as spikes, water, moving platforms, and multiple enemy types

Features

  • Single and multiplayer capability
  • Variety of power ups
  • Handul of levels
  • Speedrun timer
  • Saved best time
  • Variety of hazards/enemies
  • Fast and dynamic player controller

Project Design Goals

  • Design a simple yet fun game to allow our programmer to adjust to Unity
  • Design a game that had fast-paced gameplay
  • Design a game that also included some form of combat
  • Design levels that use the power ups to get an advantage

Tech

  • Platform: Windows PC
  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Tools Used: Unity, Plastic, Google docs, Discord, Confluence, Jira
  • Team Size: 6
  • Role: Project Lead, Game Producer, Game Designer, Gameplay Programmer, System Programmer, Sound Designer
